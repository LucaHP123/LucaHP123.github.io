SKIING IN THE MOVIES

My project is titled Skiing in the Movies. As an avid skier, most of my thoughts are consumed by skiing. This interactive story project for my Western Washington University Design class was an opportunity to combine my passion for skiing and film. Growing up, my dad would quiz me on 102.5, the classic rock station, and recommend many movies he saw growing up, like ‘On Her Majesty’s Secret Service,’ a classic James Bond film filmed on the Jungfrau Joch in Switzerland with a fantastic Bond escape on skis. From mainstream movies like Downhill Racer (1969) and Molly Game (2017) to more obscure films like Apocalypse Snow (1983). I hope this interactive story keeps you as entertained as it kept me making it. My interactive story site is presented chronologically from the oldest movies to the newest.
